We started with Aish

Bread which in Arabic literally means life - “Aish” is a key component of Egyptian culture that also symbolizes friendship, love, and loyalty. Breadfast was founded in 2017 by Mostafa Amin, Muhammad Habib and Abdallah Nofal on this concept. We are passionate about sourcing the best quality ingredients around the world.

Producing the finest products, and delivering happiness straight to the comfort of the customers’ homes- whenever and wherever they want. Ultimately at the heart of the company, is a purpose to provide value, joy, and fresh food that is good for the Egyptian soul.
