Frequently Asked Questions

What is Breadfast?

Breadfast is a one-stop-shop that provides daily fresh groceries to your doorstep. Think of Breadfast as a little supermarket in your pocket. We provide your daily shopping essentials from dairy, eggs, fresh bread, fruits and vegetables, poultry and meats, pantry staples, all the way to laundry detergents, baby products, stationary items, grooming and personal care kits. We’re continuously adding more products and more household supplies to our app.

Is Breadfast a supermarket?

Breadfast is a full-fledged supermarket app, without the long lines. We provide everything you can get from your supermarket from bakeries, groceries, and household goods, minus the long cashier lines.A one-stop app for all your daily essentials, Breadfast provides fresh bread, dairy, eggs, fruits and vegetables, poultry and meats, pantry staples, all the way to household supplies, including laundry detergents, baby products, stationary items, grooming, and personal care kits.

Where does Breadfast deliver?

We cover most neighborhoods in Cairo including Dokki, Mohandessin, Maadi, 6th of October, Sheikh Zayed, Al Rehab City, New Cairo, Madinaty, El Shorouk, Mokattam, Nasr City, Heliopolis, and Obour. We’re expanding to cover more neighborhoods across the city. Place your order anytime during the day and choose whether to get it immediately or next day.

What can I order from Breadfast?

Use Breadfast to shop for all your daily groceries from fresh bread, dairy, eggs, poultry and meats, all the way to household supplies, including laundry detergents, baby products, stationary, grooming and personal care kits.
